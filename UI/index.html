How it works

Voters

  • Create an account or sign in
  • View all politicians who are vying for certain posts
  • Vote for one politician on each post
  • View your voting activity in your profile page

Politicians

  • Create an account and mark that you are a politician
  • Select post you are interested in vying
  • Vote for certain
  • View your voting activity in your profile page
